Public Consultation on the ICT Accessibility Standard is now live!

  • The Government of Canada (GC) is excited to announce our public consultation on the ICT Accessibility Standard is now live! To participate, please provide your feedback by completing our feedback submission form (Link to come) or by attending one of our Live Sessions (Link to come)
  • Before submitting your feedback, make sure to read the draft ICT Accessibility Standard (Link to come)
  • The consultation will end on Date and time. Following this date, we will no longer be accepting feedback
  • If you have any questions or concerns about our consultation, or would like to provide your feedback in an alternative way, please email us at PolitiqueNumerique@tbs-sct.gc.ca
