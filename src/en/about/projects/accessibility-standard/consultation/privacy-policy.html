Privacy Notice Statement (registration form)

The Treasury Board of Canada Secretariat’s (TBS) Digital Policy Division Team is conducting a series of consultations on the draft Standard on Information and Communications Technology (ICT) Accessibility. The purpose of these consultations is to collect feedback from subject matter experts, persons with disabilities and the public on the draft Standard. The goal of the draft Standard is to help make the Government of Canada’s ICT usable by all, as prescribed in the Accessibility Strategy for the Public Service.

Registration and participation to the live event consultations are voluntary. Consultation sessions with participants will be conducted using Zoom, where participants will be recorded; participants may leave a session at any time. For additional information, please refer to Zoom’s Privacy Statement and Terms of Service. You may contact our team at DigitalPolicy.PolitiqueNumerique-Consultation@tbs-sct.gc.ca if you have concerns about video/recording to discuss alternative options.

The personal information provided via the registration form is collected under the authority of the Financial Administration Act and is subject to the Access to Information Act and the Privacy Act. Failure to complete all required fields on the registration form will inhibit our ability to send you the virtual event details, which will prevent your participation to the selected live events. By choosing to register to participate in these live event consultations, you may receive emails with information related to your participation and be asked to complete a follow up survey. The Digital Policy Division team may publish a summary of what was learned from our feedback submission form and live events, including quotes or narratives on the consultation website and Open Government Portal. Participant’s names will not be associated with responses, quotes, or narratives, unless informed consent is sought and obtained by the person or persons to whom the information relates to program officials for its disclosure. The engagement activities and dissemination of information to support this initiative is described in the Standard Personal Information Banks Public Communications (PSU 935) and Outreach Activities (PSU 938).

Under the Privacy Act, you have the right to the correction of, access to, and protection of your personal information. If you have any questions about this privacy statement, please contact the TBS Access to Information and Privacy Coordinator by telephone at 1-866-312-1511 or by email to ATIP.AIPRP@tbs-sct.gc.ca. If you are not satisfied with TBS’s response to your privacy concern, you may wish to contact the Office of the Privacy Commissioner.

Audio recording consent

This live event will be recorded and presented over Zoom for the purposes of feedback evaluation and analysis. By agreeing to participate and engage in this live event, you understand that:

  • Any comments you may make during the event will be recorded.
  • You must not provide any identifiable information about yourself or others.
  • Your responses will not be confidential due to your audio likeness in the recording.
